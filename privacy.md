**Effective date:** 3 May 2026

## 1. Who we are

**Laba** is a mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you use the App.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account data (via Clerk)

When you sign in, **Clerk** (our authentication provider) may process:

- your **email address** (including for email verification / sign-in);
- optional **first and last name** if you provide them;
- optional **profile image** (uploaded by you or supplied by Google if you use Google Sign-In);
- **session tokens** needed to keep you signed in securely.

Clerk’s own privacy practices apply to how they process data on our behalf. See Clerk’s documentation for details.

### 2.2 Data on your device

The App may store preferences and light app state on your device using **AsyncStorage** and **Secure Store** (for example: theme preference, favourite stations, playback-related state). This data stays on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

### 2.3 Streaming and analytics

When you play a stream, your device connects to **third-party servers** operated by broadcasters or their partners (in Uganda and, where included, other countries). We do not control those servers. The App may use standard networking as needed to deliver audio/video.

## 3. How we use information

We use the information above to:

- authenticate you and maintain your session;
- display your profile in the App;
- remember your preferences (such as theme and favourites);
- operate, secure, and improve the App.

We **do not sell** your personal information.

## 4. Sharing

We share data only as needed to operate the App, including with:

- **Clerk** (authentication);
- **Google** (if you choose Google Sign-In);
- **broadcasters / streaming infrastructure** when you play content.

We do not share your data for unrelated marketing.

## 5. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. In the App you can:

- view and update profile details under **Settings → Manage account**;
- **delete your account** permanently from the same screen (Danger zone), when enabled.

You may also contact us at **ywalum@gmail.com** for privacy-related requests.

## 6. Children

The App is **not directed at children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard practices appropriate to a consumer mobile app (including secure session handling via Clerk). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. International transfers

Clerk and other providers may process data in regions outside Uganda. By using the App, you understand that such processing may occur.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy by posting a new version on this page. The effective date at the top will change when we do.
